Beginner Tips

Simple rules that make learning easier.

Do not memorize everything

Understand the idea, then search when you forget syntax. Real developers search too.

Build small projects

One small finished project teaches more than many unfinished tutorials.

Read errors slowly

Error messages usually point to the file, line, or concept causing the problem.

Practice layout first

Master headings, sections, cards, navigation, forms, and responsive design.
